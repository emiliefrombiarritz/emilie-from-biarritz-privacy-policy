Privacy Policy

Emilie From Biarritz Recipes Agent

Effective date: July 23, 2026

Emilie From Biarritz respects privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Emilie From Biarritz Recipes Agent processes information when connecting to Pinterest and publishing content through the official Pinterest API.

1. About the application

Emilie From Biarritz Recipes Agent is a private content automation tool hosted on Amazon Web Services.

It is used exclusively by the owner of the Emilie From Biarritz Pinterest Business account to prepare, schedule and publish organic Pins promoting family cookbooks and recipes.

The application is not offered to the general public and does not provide accounts or services to customers or third parties.

2. Information processed

When the owner connects the application to Pinterest, the application may process:

  • Pinterest account identifiers;
  • Pinterest board names and board identifiers;
  • Pin identifiers and publication status;
  • Pin titles, descriptions, alternative text and destination links;
  • Images selected and approved for publication;
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to access the Pinterest API;
  • Technical logs relating to executions, errors and publication results.

The application does not request, collect or store the user's Pinterest password.

3. How information is used

Information is used only to:

  • Authenticate the owner's Pinterest account;
  • Retrieve the owner's Pinterest boards;
  • Publish approved organic Pins;
  • Verify whether a Pin was successfully published;
  • Avoid publishing the same content more than once;
  • Record publication dates and statuses;
  • Diagnose technical errors and maintain application security.

Information is not used for behavioural advertising, profiling, data brokerage or automated decision-making concerning individuals.

4. Pinterest access

The application accesses only the Pinterest account explicitly authorised by its owner through Pinterest's official OAuth authentication process.

It does not access Pins, boards or account information belonging to unrelated Pinterest users.

The application may request permission to:

  • Read the authenticated owner's boards;
  • Read the authenticated owner's Pins;
  • Create and publish Pins on the authenticated owner's account.

The use of Pinterest is also governed by Pinterest's own terms and privacy policy.

5. Storage and security

Application data is stored in the owner's private Amazon Web Services account.

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are used, including:

  • Restricted AWS Identity and Access Management permissions;
  • Private Amazon S3 storage;
  • Encrypted transmission using HTTPS;
  • Secure storage of API credentials and OAuth tokens;
  • Access limited to the application owner and authorised AWS services;
  • Technical logs used only for security, maintenance and troubleshooting.

Pinterest access tokens, refresh tokens, application secrets and AWS credentials are never intentionally published in public source-code repositories.

6. Sharing of information

Information processed by the application is not sold, rented or shared for marketing purposes.

Information may be processed by providers strictly necessary to operate the workflow, including:

  • Pinterest, for authentication, board retrieval and Pin publication;
  • Amazon Web Services, for hosting, storage, logs and automated execution;
  • Amazon, when a Pinterest user follows a link to a cookbook product page.

These providers process information in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies.

Information may also be disclosed when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect the application, its owner or its users.

7. Data retention

Pinterest board and Pin identifiers may be retained for as long as necessary to manage publication history and prevent duplicate publications.

Technical logs are retained only for a reasonable period required for security, maintenance and troubleshooting.

OAuth credentials are retained while the Pinterest connection remains active. They are deleted or invalidated when access is revoked or when the application is discontinued.

8. Revoking access and deleting information

The owner may revoke the application's access through the connected Pinterest account settings.

A request to delete information stored by the application may also be submitted using the contact details below.

Following a valid request, locally stored Pinterest identifiers, publication history and authentication credentials will be deleted, unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.

9. International processing

The services used to operate the application may process information in countries outside the user's country of residence.

Where applicable, service providers are responsible for implementing appropriate safeguards for international transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Children's privacy

The application is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The cookbook content may relate to family meals, but the application does not create accounts for, identify or collect personal information from children.

11. Privacy rights

Depending on applicable data protection law, individuals may have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal information;
  • Correct inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion;
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;
  • Request portability where applicable;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Individuals may also contact their competent data protection authority. In France, this is the Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés, commonly known as the CNIL.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the application, Pinterest API requirements, Amazon Web Services or applicable laws.

The latest version will remain available on this page, together with its effective date.
