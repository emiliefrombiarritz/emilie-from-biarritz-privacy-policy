About the application

Emilie From Biarritz Recipes Agent is a private content management application hosted on Amazon Web Services.

It is used exclusively by the owner of the Emilie From Biarritz Pinterest Business account to prepare, review, schedule and publish organic Pins promoting the Emilie's Little Recipes cookbook series.

The application uses the official Pinterest API and is not offered as a service to third parties.
